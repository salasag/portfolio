About

A CSV of Nintendo stock data is converted into blocks for Mario to jump around on and hopefully make some money through investing. Ideally, by playing this game, players find that one of the best things you can do when investing is to stay in the market for a long time and to minimize mistakes.

Controls

Use the arrow keys to move and jump, press space to flip your position, and the R-key resets the game.

Making Money

If the next block is higher (stock went up) you make money proportional to the increase. Likewise if it goes down you lose money. This can be reversed (similar to shorting a stock) by pressing space bar which flips Mario to the other side of the blocks. Avoid falling off the screen as you will lose 10 percent of your money! Jumping on Goombas will give you 3% of your money. However, be careful as they can knock Mario over the edge if their sides are hit!
